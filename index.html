An e-Visa for Turkey is a digital travel authorization allowing entry and travel within the country, serving as an alternative to traditional visa processes. It's obtained online through a simple application process that includes providing necessary information and paying with a credit or debit card. Upon successful application, a link to download the e-Visa is emailed, which travelers should carry in both digital and printed forms. Although it's electronically verified, entry can still be denied without explanation by Turkish officials.
